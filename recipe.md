# egg_omlet_recipe

Recipe:


Break the egg and put the yolk in a dry bowl. 
Chop some coriander leaves into thin slices and keep it aside.
Heat a pan and put the yolk of the egg in it.
Put some salt and pepper on it.
Put the grated cheese and chopped coriander on it.
Take a spachula and turn it till some parts of the omlet turn till brown in colour and put in a plate.
Serve HOT!!!
